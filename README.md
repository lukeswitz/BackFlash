---

Drag events into the right spot on the timeline. Green = correct. Red = it snaps to where it belongs. 8 cards per game, no repeats.

Every card is a front-page moment: assassinations, viral memes, infamous heists, space missions, natural disasters, tech launches, legendary sports moments, celebrity scandals, ancient battles, and more. 700+ hand-curated events across every era from the Trojan War to the Titan sub implosion.

No two consecutive cards share a category. No year cheats -- dates are stripped from descriptions.

---

One HTML file. No frameworks. No build step. No backend. No accounts. No telemetry.

```bash
open index.html
```
